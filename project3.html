Andry.

This is Miss Andry Lindell: conspiracy theorist, criminal idolizer and a soon-to-be-dead girl. She's the protagonist of a comic (currently under the working title Unadherent) set in an early 2000s cyberpunk milieu.

I designed her to exude personality with relatively little detail. This way, she's easy to draw repeatedly, which I consider important in comic book character design.

This 3D model was part of my bachelor's thesis, where I created it to be used in marketing.
