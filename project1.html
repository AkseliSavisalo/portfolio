Decks.

  • The Smug Moon

  • Pulse

  • Booty reaper

In 2019, I created these illustrations for a group called Sekoittamo, who were designing electrically driven skateboards.

Pulse turned out to be my least favorite of the three illustrations, because it almost feels like it means something. On the other hand, I love Booty Reaper's nonsense design and its alabaster-and-ink color palette.

To our absolute dismay, Sekoittamo went defunct as a result of the pandemic. The electric skateboard project also died before a single board was finished.
