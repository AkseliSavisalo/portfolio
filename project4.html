Widowbat.

This was a personal project for the purpose of learning 3D- and game design skills.
The modding scene for Arkham Knignt seemed to feature surprisingly few fully original 3D models, which made this project an appealing challenge. I spent time landing on a design which would be distinct without looking out of place in the game environment. I also made fully custom wings, a removable helmet, and small moving parts for cinematics, also things I rarely found in other mods.

_Widowbat can be found on Nexusmods_

Hot take: Arkham Knight is the best game in the series.
